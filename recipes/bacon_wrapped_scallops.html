Bacon Wrapped Scallops

Ingredients

Original recipe (1X) yields 4 servings

  • 6 slices bacon
  • 12 sea scallops, rinsed and drained
  • 3 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F(174 degrees C)
  2. Cut bacon slices in half and wrap each half around a scallop. Use a tooth pick to secure in place. Drizzle lemon juice over scallops. Place on a cookie sheet.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until bacon is cooked. Server warm.
