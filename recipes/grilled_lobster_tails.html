Grilled Lobster Tails

Ingredients

Original recipe (1X) yields 2 servings

  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2(10 ounce) rock lobster tails

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Pour lemon juice into a small bowl; slowly whisk in olive oil. Whisk in salt, paprika, white pepper, and garlic powder until combined.
  4. Split lobster tails lengthwise with a large knife, then brush flesh with some marinade.
  5. Place lobster tails flesh-side down on the preheated grill and cook, turning once and basting frequently with marinade, until opaque and firm to touch, 10 to 12 minutes. Discard any remaining marinade.
